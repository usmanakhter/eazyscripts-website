Take a look at our services

E-Prescribing

Our e-Prescribing software allows physicians to submit electronic prescriptions, at pharmacies convenient for their patients, right from their mobile device. With SureScripts Certification, connecting Physicians to 90% of Pharmacies in the United States, EazyScripts offers the most robust 1-touch e-Prescribing software around!

Refill Request

Seamless refill request with a touch of a button, connecting you to your Physician immediately. This powerful feature will eliminate the need for wait times during a phone call. Make a request on the go!


Electronic Prior Authorization

No more waiting for insurance authorization! EazyScripts e-PA offers an intuitive electronic prior authorization tool so that Providers can quickly and seamlessly treat their patients. By streamlining the entire Prior Authorization process with patient insurance companies, EazyScripts saves you invaluable time.

Surescripts Certification

With our award-winning Surescripts Certification, you get access to over 90% of USA Pharmacies, over 30,000 FDA approved prescriptions, and a fully secure electronic submission tool to the Pharmacy of your choice, among many more features.


Customizable Integration

Our platform is built to integrate effortlessly with any Health IT system, allowing for secure information exchange and countless customizable integration options. Whether you want a new user interface or powerful feature add-ons, EazyScripts will get the job done.

Mobile Friendly

Point-of-care prescribing is a must, and in this day and age, that means mobile device support. EazyScripts gives you the prescribing power you need on the go. With just a click on your mobile phone, iPad, or tablet, you can send electronic prescriptions wherever convenient.
